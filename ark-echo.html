The Story

Long before our recorded history, there existed Earth 1.0 — a significantly larger world that orbited in what is now the asteroid belt. Its inhabitants were giants, many standing 30 to 50 feet tall, living among forests so immense that their petrified trunks later became what we call mountains.

This is a personal synthesis of fringe lunar theories, ancient astronaut ideas, reincarnation concepts, and one vivid personal dream.
Presented purely as speculative storytelling and late-night conversation fuel — not as established fact.
